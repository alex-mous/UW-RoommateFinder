About RoommateFinder

Created by Alex Mous, Pedro Amarante, Nahush Shrivatsa and Palak Mehta, UW RoommateFinder is a project designed to help incoming students to the University of Washington find roommates who have similar preferences and interests. Please contact any one of us through GitHub! Our usernames are @alex-mous, @pedrodeoliamarante, @nahushvatsa and @pmehta124!
